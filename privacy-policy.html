Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

1. Who We Are

INTERACTION is operated by PINERA Corp. ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the INTERACTION app.

Contact: support@pinera.app

2. Information We Collect

  • Email address (used for account authentication)
  • Display name, age, gender, and bio (profile information you provide)
  • Profile photo (uploaded by you)
  • Approximate location (to show nearby users — precise coordinates are stored temporarily and not shared with other users)
  • Messages sent within the app (automatically deleted after 5 minutes)
  • Device token (for push notifications)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To show your profile to nearby users within your chosen radius
  • To enable real-time messaging between users
  • To send push notifications for new messages
  • To enforce our community safety features (blocking, reporting)
  • To improve the app and diagnose issues

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Firebase (Google) — for authentication, database, storage, and push notifications. Firebase's privacy policy
  • Apple — for Sign in with Apple authentication.

We do not share your email address or precise location with other users.

5. Message Expiry

All messages sent in INTERACTION automatically expire and are permanently deleted from our servers after 5 minutes. This is a core feature of the app and cannot be disabled. Neither party can recover expired messages.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Messages: deleted automatically after 5 minutes
  • Profile data and account: retained until you delete your account
  • Deleting your account permanently removes all your data from our systems, including your profile, photo, and any associated records

To delete your account, go to Settings → Delete Account in the app.

7. Location Data

Your location is used only to find other INTERACTION users in your vicinity. We store approximate coordinates to power this feature. Your precise location is never displayed to other users — they only see that you are "nearby."

You can disable location access at any time in iOS Settings → INTERACTION → Location.

8. Children's Privacy

INTERACTION is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 17. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us at support@pinera.app and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@pinera.app.

10. Security

We use Firebase's industry-standard security infrastructure. All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS. We implement access controls to restrict data to authorised users only.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, email us at support@pinera.app.
